Explore Venice and its surroundings

Embark on an unforgettable boat trip across the Venetian lagoon to discover Venice and its surrounding islands!

The main places you'll visit

  • Gran Canal. The main waterway running through Venice.
  • Murano Island. Renowned worldwide for its blown glass glassware.
  • Burano Island. Famous for its lacework and brightly colored houses.
  • Torcello Island. Full of historical significance as the birthplace of Venice.
